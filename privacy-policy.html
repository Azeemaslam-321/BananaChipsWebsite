Privacy Policy

How RudraBitez handles your information.

RudraBitez collects only the information needed to respond to enquiries, process orders, and support customers. This may include your name, phone number, email address, delivery details, and messages you send through forms or WhatsApp.

How We Use Information

  • To respond to product, bulk, and distributor enquiries.
  • To confirm, process, or support orders and delivery discussions.
  • To send updates related to your enquiry, purchase, or subscribed communication.

Sharing Of Data

Your information is not sold. It may only be shared with service providers or logistics/payment partners when necessary to fulfill an order or support your request.
